Lodging Information

The 2009 Linux Plumbers Conference will be held at the Portland Marriott Downtown Waterfront in Portland, Oregon.

The Linux Plumbers Conference (via the Linux Foundation) has secured room blocks and discounted room rates at the Marriott hotel and encourage you to stay on-site to take full advantage of the Linux Plumbers Conference:

Portland Marriott Downtown Waterfront (online discount)
1401 SW Naito Parkway, Portland, OR USA
+1 503 226 7600
$143/night for King or Double Rooms (Note the new lowered room rate of $143!)

You can book your room online by using the above link (it should automatically populate the group discount code linlina for you) or by calling the number above and asking for the Linux Group Rate. The cut-off for accepting hotel reservations into the room block with the negotiated rate is August 20, 2009 or until the block is filled (whichever comes first). Applicable state and local taxes currently are 12.5% (subject to change without notice). A credit card is required to hold your reservation.

If your company has a corporate rate at this hotel, you will still be able to book within The Linux Foundation block using your corporate rate.
